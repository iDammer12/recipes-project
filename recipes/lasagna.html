Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • ground meat
  • onion
  • garlic
  • rotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • egg
  • bechamel sauce
  • herbs and spices
  • basil or parsley

how to make lasagna

  1. preheat oven to 375 F
  2. bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  3. cook the lasagna noodles in said water
  4. drain and lay the noodles flat on a clean surface
  5. in a large skillet, heat some olive oil until warm
  6. add chopped onions and minced garlic and cook for another minute
  7. add ground meat and cook until brown
  8. pour the tomato sauce and season
  9. simmer for about 15-20 minutes
  10. in a bowl, combine ricotta cheese grated parmesan and an egg and mix well
  11. in a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom
  12. place a layer of lasagna noodles
  13. spread a portion of the ricotta mixture
  14. add a layer of mozzarella cheese
  15. repeat these steps until all ingredients are finished
  16. bake for about 25-30 minutes
  17. serve and enjoy!
